# 01 - Factorial

**Challenge**: Create a function that calculates the factorial of a number. The factorial of a non-negative integer $n$ is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to $n$.

**Example:**

Input: `5`
Output: `120`

**Explanation:**
$5 \times 4 \times 3 \times 2 \times 1 = 120$
